About Us

Sirius Learning Hub is a professional training institute committed to delivering high-quality education in health and safety as well as modern skill-based courses. We specialize in internationally recognized qualifications such as NEBOSH, IOSH, and OSHA, along with in-demand skills including Graphic Designing, Digital Marketing, Resin Art, and Amazon Virtual Assistant. Our mission is to equip students with practical knowledge, industry-relevant skills, and career-focused training that enables them to succeed in both local and international markets. We emphasize real-world learning, expert guidance, and continuous support to ensure high success rates for our students. At Sirius Learning Hub, we believe in building confident professionals who can excel in their careers and contribute effectively to workplace safety and digital innovation.

We provide NEBOSH, IOSH, and OSHA training with practical approach and high success rate.
